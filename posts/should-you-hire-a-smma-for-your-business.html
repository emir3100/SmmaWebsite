Should You Hire a SMMA for Your Business?

Emir Jasarevic

09/24/2022

Considering hiring a Social Media Marketing Agency (SMMA)? We are going to discuss whether you as a business owner should hire a SMMA. It all depends on where you are currently in your business stage and if your business is scalable.

Sure you can learn everything by yourself and do the marketing on your own, and you should definitely do that in the early stages of your business. It is essential as a business owner to have at least the basic knowledge about marketing because when you hire a SMMA you will have the knowledge about the things they do and in extension that will help you find an actual good SMMA.

Before hiring a SMMA you need to have a product/service that you are making decent money with and can be scalable, then you should hire a SMMA. As a business owner myself I understand you want to be part of everything in your business, but to properly scale your business and make even more money you need to outsource work and hire others to do that work, that way you can focus on the company growth while leaving the experts to take care of the marketing.

When it comes to finding a great SMMA that you can work with, there are a couple of things you should look for.

Great Fit for Your Business

Nowadays, there are many agencies and finding a good one can be difficult. It can be challenging to find an SMMA that is the ideal fit for your company because it depends on the services they provide and the services you require for your particular type of business. Given that you will be partners for a considerable amount of time, it is also important that your values align and that you get along and like each other. It is equally important for you to be good and respectful just as it is for the agency. A great way to start is hop on free calls and try to see if they meet your requirements.

Pay Attention to Details

A great way to filter out the bad agencies is to pay attention to their professionalism. Is their website professional, do they ask the right questions on the calls, are their tools and equipment high standards, do they have a track record of success, do they genuinely want your business to succeed. You should be asking yourself these questions because you need to pay close attention to the small details because a company's professionalism will be reflected in how well its marketing techniques perform in most cases.

Are They Being Reasonable

If they meet all of the requirements, surely there will be an expensive service cost? Most certainly, but keep in mind that they will bring you far more in revenue and their service fee just represents a small portion of that. Simply ensure that it is an affordable service fee and it is worth it. If they give a money-back guarantee if they fall short of expectations, then it's even better.
