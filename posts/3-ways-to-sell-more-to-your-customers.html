3 Ways To Sell More To Your Customers

Emir Jasarevic

09/21/2022

Having trouble selling to your customers? We all have run into this issue at some point in our business. In this blog post we are going to cover 3 ways you can implement today for your business to start selling more.

Know Your Customer

It is essential to understand your customer to be able to sell to them. You need to enter the conversation they already have in their mind, and be the answer to their problems. Understand their pain and the core desire they have.

Hook, Story, Offer

Hook, Story, Offer is a great way to convert leads into customers. It can be used in many ways such as: websites, advertisement, speechâ€¦

Hook is the attention you are grabbing. It must be very captivating because most people don't have a high attention span nowadays. After grabbing their attention you must create a story that resonates with them and influences their emotions. The goal of the story is to increase the perceived value of what you are offering. To achieve that you need to be an attractive character and connect with your audience to serve them.

And finally present the offer that will solve their problem in other words Call To Action. This can either be selling something, or subscribing to a podcast, or email newsletterâ€¦

If an ad or a funnel doesn't work it is always because of the hook, story and offer. If you hook someone and they read your story and still don't buy, it's either because your story doesn't increase the perceived value or your offer isn't good enough.

Increase the Value of Your Offer

If you believe that your offer isn't good enough, you need to make it so good that the customers will feel stupid not buying it. Now it all depends on the type of business you have, but you need to think what more can I add to increase the value. Software is the best way to implement into your business to increase the perceived value. Depending on the complexity or your own skills, you can create software pretty inexpensively. Look at your business and try to find parts that can be automated and create software for that. This will increase the value drastically since most customers look at softwares as high value products.

Bonus

If you have a winning product that is selling but has more potential, then it is a good idea to consider hiring a SMMA because they are the experts in their field and know what they are doing.
